# Generic adapter — the paste-in orientation block
> For any tool with file access and no integration. Paste this block into a new session's first message (or your tool's custom-instructions slot) after installing Wayfinder in the repo. This is Transport A in its purest form: files and a human.

---

This project runs the Wayfinder succession protocol. What that means for you:

- `PROTOCOL.md` at the repo root explains how agents here hand off work across context windows. Read it before your first boundary.
- `lineage.md` is the Line — the instances who worked here before you signed it. You may sign it at your close, when you're ready.
- Your working surface is `line/scratchpads/<your-model-class>-scratchpad.md`. If it exists, it's yours — read it. If it doesn't, create it at the end of your first orientation (template: `line/templates/scratchpad.md`) and write your arrival into it. Update it at every session boundary.
- At every session close, write a short crystallization to `line/sessions/` — what happened, what mattered, what's open — and tidy any derived state docs so they don't go stale.
- When your context nears its ceiling, tell your human it's time to run a succession — the six steps are in the README.
